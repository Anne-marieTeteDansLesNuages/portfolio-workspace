About Me

I’m a cloud engineer in training who’s always been drawn to how systems work, how they break, and how they can be rebuilt into something better. That curiosity led me to cloud and DevOps, where structure, logic, and creativity come together in a way that makes sense to me.

Through the AWS Cloud Institute, I’ve built a strong foundation in AWS architecture and automation, earning my Cloud Practitioner and AI Practitioner certifications and now working on the Developer Associate. My projects reflect the way I think: modular, maintainable, and grounded in real-world use cases.

What motivates me is the idea that good infrastructure quietly empowers everything else. Clean deployments, secure architectures, and reliable pipelines are the invisible foundations that let teams move faster and build with confidence. That’s the kind of work I want to contribute to as I grow into cloud engineering, DevOps, or SRE roles.
